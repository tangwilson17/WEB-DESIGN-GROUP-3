Deluxe Room

Deluxe Guest rooms are decorated elegantly and contemporary in natural colours featuring dark hardwood floors. These spacious rooms offer a comfortable work space. The warm travertine stone bathroom, some with a deep soak bathtub, features a large rainfall shower, luxurious amenities, micro cotton towels and LCD mirror TV. Deluxe Guest Rooms sleep up to two adults and there are multiple connecting options available.

A Charming 5* Luxury Hotel & Beautiful

It would be impossible to mention 1908 Lisboa Hotel without taking a deeper look at the fabulous building that houses it. A 1908 award-winning building is reborn in XXI’s century Lisbon. When architect Adães Bermudes sketched the lot on the corner between Largo do Intendente and Av. Almirante Reis, he could not have imagined that 108 years later someone would pick his plans, rebuild his masterpiece and complete his project. After witnessing the decline of its neighbourhood in the late twentieth century, and becoming home, guest house, retailer and even lottery shop, it is time for the building to turn into a hotel and finally boast the splendour of its façade, proud of its war wounds, ready to help Portuguese and foreigners fall in love with the city.

Deluxe Room

Deluxe Guest rooms are decorated elegantly and contemporary in natural colours featuring dark hardwood floors. These spacious rooms offer a comfortable work space. The warm travertine stone bathroom, some with a deep soak bathtub, features a large rainfall shower, luxurious amenities, micro cotton towels and LCD mirror TV. Deluxe Guest Rooms sleep up to two adults and there are multiple connecting options available.

Luxury Room

Smart hospitality with amazing mountain views, fine fabrics, art and wooden furnishings, these rooms offer Pirin Mountain view. Relax in the cozy double or twin bed or enjoy a glass of wine on your private balcony and unwind Occupancy: 2 adults plus extra person accommodated in a rollaway bed.

King's Room

This is the perfect suite to accommodate a family! It offers two separate bedrooms, with a double bed and bed respectively and a bathroom with a walk-in shower. The spacious living room also features a dining creating a perfect area for enjoying private meals and family activities with amazing view to the Pirin and forest. Occupancy: 2 adults & 2 children – all in private bedrooms .

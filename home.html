Enjoy a Luxury Experience

On a budget in Seattle or looking for something different? The fancy charm and "Home away from home" atmosphere of The Nothern will be sure to make you feel like one of the family.

Guest Amenities: Internet access, mini fridge and microwave, Laundry facilities available, Flat screen cable television, Ice and vending machines located in the lobby, Coffee maker and hairdryers in all guestrooms.

Room service available seven days a week from the Rhododendron Cafe, Limited wheelo hair accessibility, Guest library and business center, Printing & fax services available, 100% non-smoking and pet free, Rooms are not air conditioned - fans are available, Self-parking available at Virginia Mason hospital for a fee.

Deluxe Room

Deluxe Guest rooms are decorated elegantly and contemporary in natural colours featuring dark hardwood floors. These spacious rooms offer a comfortable work space. The warm travertine stone bathroom, some with a deep soak bathtub, features a large rainfall shower, luxurious amenities. Deluxe Guest Rooms sleep up to two adults and there are multiple connecting options available.

Luxury Room

Smart hospitality with amazing mountain views, fine fabrics, art and wooden furnishings, these rooms offer Pirin Mountain view. Relax in the cozy double or twin bed or enjoy a glass of wine on your private balcony and unwind Occupancy: 2 adults plus extra person accommodated in a rollaway bed.

King's Room

This is the perfect suite to accommodate a family! It offers two separate bedrooms, with a double bed and bed respectively and a bathroom with a walk-in shower. The spacious living room also features a dining creating a perfect area for enjoying private meals and family activities with amazing view to the Pirin and forest.

Memorable Event

For each event, impressive locations and white-glove service are available.

Northen Lagoon Hotel is the ideal location for hosting a business conference, hosting a milestone celebration, or planning the wedding of your dreams We have 11 elegant event venues with 2,628 square metres of polished space, free high-speed WiFi, and cutting-edge technology.
